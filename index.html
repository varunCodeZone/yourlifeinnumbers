About This Project

Your Life in Numbers is an interactive web app that calculates fun life stats based on your birthdate. It shows:

  • 💓 Estimated number of heartbeats
  • 🌬️ Total breaths taken
  • 📆 Days you’ve lived
  • 🎂 Days remaining if you live to 80

It also features a colorful doughnut chart to visualize your life's timeline. Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript & Chart.js.
